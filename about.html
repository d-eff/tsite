Originally from Florida, I received my BFA from Florida State University and moved to Brooklyn to pursue a career in Graphic Design. I worked on visual design, branding and user experience, most notably leading design for Weather Whiskers, a cat-themed weather app at Mindspark, an IAC company. This fall I began graduate school at NYU’s Interactive Telecommunications Program (ITP).

At ITP I explore the overlap of tech and craft. I incorporate my background in knitting, sewing, embroidery, and soft sculpture with my rapidly expanding knowledge of programming and electronics fabrication. I love to teach and currently run the school’s Soft Fabrication Lab, hosting weekly workshops and sewing tutorials as part of the Soft Club.
