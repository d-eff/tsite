Processes

A triptych of pieces made simultaneously using the same formula. Started in 2009 and finished in 2012.

Found images, Found objects and String

Process 0

Process 1

Process A

This work was inspired by Robert Rauschenberg’s Factum I & Factum II. His pieces are “twins” created simultaneously, challenging the idea of spontaneity in abstract expressionism. Instead of aiming to create identical works, I sought to watch three pieces evolve given the same attention over an extended period of time. The elements: collages made one winter break with a bandaged hand and a stapler, scraps of fabric from various hand-sewing projects, sign letters collected by a close friend, and many objects found on the ground during long walks through Tallahassee.

As they appeared on the weathered walls of my studio under harsh lighting.
