Weather Whiskers

Interface Design, Content Creation and Brand Identity for a cat-themed weather app on iOS and Android.
Available for free download in the Apple App Store, Google Play Store, and at WeatherWhiskers.com.

Interface Design

The interface is designed to be “so simple a cat could use it”, which means uncluttered screens that can be navigated even with large, clumsy paws. We provided visual clues about the weather like the cat’s clothing, expression, and background color.

I created custom icons, background colors and commentary to each weather condition.

Content Creation

Our app turns the mundane task of checking the weather into a delightful cat-filled experience. The cats react physically and emotionally to the environment. Our goal was to make users smile with high-quality content. The worse the weather, the more challenging the task.

I created the Tone & Voice & Meow Guide for Weather Whiskers that outlines all Weather Whiskers content including copy, emails, and social media.
